About Lumiere Hotel

Est. 1892

A Century of Unmatched Elegance

What began as the private mansion of the Comte de Lumiere in 1892 has evolved into one of the world's most celebrated luxury hotels. Every corridor, every suite, every detail carries the weight of history and the promise of perfection.

Through the Belle Epoque, two World Wars, and the modernisation of Paris, Lumiere has stood as an unwavering beacon of hospitality — hosting royalty, presidents, artists, and travellers who seek nothing short of the extraordinary.
