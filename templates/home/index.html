Our Story

A Legacy of Luxury Since 1892

For over a century, Lumiere Hotel has been synonymous with Parisian elegance. Originally the private mansion of the Comte de Lumiere, every stone whispers stories of royalty, diplomacy, and romance.

Today, we continue that tradition — blending the grandeur of the 19th century with the comforts of the modern age, offering guests an experience that transcends the ordinary.
